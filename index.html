Services

The Club ELD app is your advanced fleet management software solution. It is a mobile E-log, developed to break you free from hassles and mistakes involved in manual logging of driver’s hours of service (HOS) and duty statuses.

Compliance

Club ELD ensures streamline and automate compliance, including HOS, ELD, DOT, DVIR and more, making it easy for drivers to stay up to date with the current regulations.

All-In-One App

Track, store, and send your logs to authorized services with the help of a comprehensive set of tools provided by the Club ELD application.

Team Driving

Club ELD allows drivers to work in pairs using the same app installed on the same device.

Load Board

With the Load Board interface, fleet dispatchers can offer you cargoes directly in your Club ELD application.

24\7 Support

In case you face any problems with the app or ELD device, you can always contact the Club ELD support team.

Friendly Interface

The Club ELD app was designed to be easy to use from the very first try and it does not require any special training to start working with the app
